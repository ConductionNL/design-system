How it works

From your data to your users, in one pipeline.

Drop your existing data in. Nextcloud handles auth and storage. Four ConNext apps shape it into a service. Your users see a portal, an API, or both.

OpenConnector Pulls schemas, REST, SOAP, files. One adapter per source, point it, name it, ship.
OpenRegister Validates against schema, stores typed records. Versioned, audit-logged, queryable.
OpenCatalogi Publishes datasets with metadata. WOO-ready, OData-shaped, public or scoped.
MyDash Renders the consumer view. Per-tenant theming, NLDS-compliant, embeddable.
