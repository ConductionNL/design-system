Why we built it this way

One layout, three tones, not three templates

Reminders are one template with a level-pill at the top. Layout, branding and chrome stay the same; only the body text and the level pill change. The client recognises the document instantly, even on repeat reads.

  • Three pills, one active. 14d / 30d / 60d. The active pill is KNVB orange; the other two stay cobalt-50. One-glance signal of "what stage am I in".
  • Invoice block with orange accent. Left edge cobalt → orange as a visual tell that this is "action required". Respects one-orange-per-screen.
  • Tone escalates, language doesn't. No collection-agency formality at level 1 or 2. Legal escalation only mentioned at level 3. For MKB buyers, friendly-but-persistent works better than firm-from-the-start.
  • No "hope you're well". MKB-direct. Subject is the problem; the opening is about the invoice, not the weather.
  • One concrete action per level. Call, pay, arrange. No menu of four options.
