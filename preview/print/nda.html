Article 1 · confidential information

Confidential information means all data provided or shown by one party to the other, orally or in writing, in any form. Publicly known information and information lawfully obtained without an obligation of confidentiality are excluded.

Article 2 · confidentiality

Both parties undertake to keep confidential information strictly secret. The information is used only for the purpose for which it was provided and not disclosed to third parties without prior written consent.

Article 3 · personnel

Confidential information is shared only with personnel who need to know it for the cooperation. Both parties ensure their personnel are bound by the same confidentiality obligation.

Article 4 · term

This agreement is effective from the date of signing and ends 24 months after the cooperation ends. The confidentiality obligation remains in force after this period to the extent reasonably necessary.

Article 5 · law and disputes

Dutch law applies to this agreement. Disputes are submitted in first instance to the competent court in Amsterdam, unless the parties agree otherwise in the meantime.

Why we built it this way

One page, readable without a lawyer

An NDA isn't the bottleneck of an MKB deal. Five articles, short, in plain English. No ten-page corporate contract; one page a director reads and signs in five minutes.

  • Mutual, not one-sided. Both parties get the same obligation. MKB buyers don't sign one-way NDAs, and we don't want one either.
  • 24 months, not "in perpetuity". Realistic term. Eternal clauses don't get enforced by courts anyway.
  • Five articles, not fifteen. Definition, confidentiality, personnel, term, law. What doesn't fit in these five doesn't belong in an NDA.
  • Two signatures, equal weight. No "on behalf of the Counterparty signed by the authorised representative of the" wording. Name, place, date, signature.
  • Dutch law, Amsterdam venue. Standard, non-negotiable. Saves three emails about jurisdiction.
