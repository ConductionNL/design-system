Apps vs Solutions, say it right
APP OpenCatalogi, software we build and ship. Concrete, installable, has a version number.
SOLUTION WOO compliance, what a customer achieves with one or more apps. A goal, not a download.
WRONG "our WOO app" · there isn't one
RIGHT "our WOO solution, built on OpenCatalogi and OpenConnector"
Never do
Stock photos of "diverse team meetings", handshakes, whiteboards.
3D renders or gradient backgrounds (except Nextcloud's official cyan-blue, on the workspace hex).
Other shades of blue on the Conduction logo.
Orange as a primary button fill, cobalt owns that.
Two orange accents on the same screen.
Flat-top hexagons. Always pointy-top.
Italics for emphasis. Use weight or color.
"Get started", "Sign up", trial-urgency CTAs.
