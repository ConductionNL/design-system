## The starting point

Four SaaS subscriptions, three vendor contracts, and a fourth on the way. The IT lead estimated 9 percent of operating budget went to per-seat licensing on tools that did not talk to each other.

## What we did

Stood up Nextcloud on the existing on-prem hardware, installed OpenRegister, OpenCatalogi, OpenConnector, DocuDesk, and MyDash from the app store. Migrated the data with two OpenConnector flows. Trained the team in two half-day sessions.

## The numbers

- Eleven weeks from kickoff to cutover
- Five apps replacing four SaaS subscriptions
- One vendor contract instead of three
- Annual savings: roughly two FTE-months of admin time, plus the licence delta
