Card uses clip-path: var(--clip-cut-tlbr-lg) so the top-left cut points back at the pitch headline · single primary CTA in KNVB orange (one orange accent per component) · no form fields, no legal copy · lead-capture form variant is reachable by passing card.children instead of card.cta.

React / MDX usage

Mount in MDX as <DownloadPanel/>

Cobalt-800 split panel with a pitch on the left and a cut-corner white card on the right. The card is form-less by default and renders a single primary CTA in KNVB orange via Button tone="orange". Reuse the same chrome for report downloads, thinkpiece links, paper-format announcements, or any "we made a thing, click here" moment that does not warrant the full lead-capture form.

Import & example

import {DownloadPanel} from '@conduction/docusaurus-preset/components';

<DownloadPanel
  title={<>This is not an app shelf.<br/>It is <span className="next-blue">Nextcloud</span> as a platform.</>}
  lede="The sovereign-workplace bundles are the start, not the finish."
  meta="Thinkpiece · 7 min · By Ruben van der Linde"
  card={{
    title: 'Want the full essay?',
    body: 'The thinkpiece on Nextcloud as a platform.',
    cta: { label: 'Read the thinkpiece', href: '/academy/the-platform-moment' },
  }}
/>

Props

  • title — pitch headline, h3, white on cobalt-800. Accepts JSX for <br/> and brand-cited spans.
  • lede — one or two sentences under the title, 78% white.
  • meta — optional small line under the lede, 55% white. Use for format, length, byline.
  • card — object: {title, body, cta, children}. cta renders an orange Button; children lets you slot a form instead.

Brand notes

  • One orange accent per component — the CTA. No second orange element inside the same panel.
  • Top-left cut on the card points back at the pitch headline. Do not flip the clip-path.
  • Card sits on white inside cobalt-800 by huisstijl rule for partner / client tiles on tinted surfaces.
