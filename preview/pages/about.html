The company

We're Conduction. We build the apps you read about on this site.

A Dutch open-source product company in Amsterdam. Twelve apps shipping under EUPL-1.2, nine people on the team, one product mission: an open ecosystem of Nextcloud apps that solve concrete problems for MKB and overheid.

Founded 2019 Amsterdam, NL Conduction B.V. KvK 76741850
What we believe

Three rules that decide what we build.

Open by default.

Every line of code we ship is EUPL-1.2 on GitHub. Issues, releases, roadmap discussions, all public. The day you uninstall is as easy as the day you installed.

Build on Nextcloud.

We don't replace your platform; we plug into the one you already log in to every morning. Files, identity, calendar, Talk, Nextcloud has it. We add the typed-data layer on top.

Concrete outcomes, not transformatie.

We won't sell you a roadmap workshop. We'll install OpenCatalogi in 2 minutes and you'll have a working catalogue this afternoon. The problem is shipping software, not strategy decks.

Origin

From consultancy to product company.

2019

Conduction B.V. founded.

Started in Amsterdam as a Dutch government consultancy, building one-off ZGW integrations for gemeenten. Realised most clients needed the same five things.

2021

Common Ground & Common Gateway.

Started building our own open-source framework for the Dutch Common Ground architecture: a Common Gateway that gemeenten could plug into instead of yet another point-to-point integration. Adopted by several municipalities.

2025

Pivot to Nextcloud.

Dropped Common Gateway as our primary stack and refocused fully on Nextcloud apps. The lessons from Common Ground, typed registers, open APIs, federated catalogues, became the foundation of OpenRegister, OpenCatalogi and OpenConnector.

2026

ConNext.

The twelve apps brought under one product brand. connext.conduction.nl launches as the public surface; Conduction stays the company name behind the contracts and the hex avatar.

Nu

Aan het bouwen.

Open roadmap on GitHub, weekly stable releases, partner program ramping up. Het verhaal is nog niet af, connext.conduction.nl krijgt elke sprint nieuwe apps en grotere klanten.
