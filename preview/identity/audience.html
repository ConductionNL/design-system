Honest about the gap · today vs the direction

Government is still the majority. MKB is the goal.

We're not pretending. Most of our current customers are government — municipalities, joint ventures — and they've been with us for years. They're welcome and they stay welcome. But Conduction's centre of gravity is shifting, and the product, the voice, and the marketing reflect that. We tell both stories without disowning either.
