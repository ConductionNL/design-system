MockScene. The overlap shot.

Sibling to AppMock, WidgetMock, and SidebarMock. AppMock paints a single product surface. WidgetMock paints one tile. SidebarMock paints one detail panel. MockScene paints them together: one sidebar centred, several widgets floating around it on a tinted cobalt-50 surface. Reach for it on cards and heroes that talk about cross-cutting integration ("typed data, surfaced everywhere").

01

OpenRegister surfaces everywhere

SCENE

A typed register at the centre with the Metadata sidebar pulled out. Mail brings the row into the inbox, Files brings it into the folder tree, the activity sparkline tracks who is reading and writing it.

02

Procest, with documentation in arm's reach

SCENE

A case at the centre with the xWiki tab pulled out, so the case-handling protocol reads next to the live case. Werkvoorraad widget shows the queue around it, due-today shows what crosses today's deadline, the case timeline traces stage progress.

03

Document workshop, signed and shipped

SCENE

A document at the centre with the Signatures tab open: signers and their status. Anonymise drop on the side, pending list keeping the document moving, a recent-runs widget showing the OpenConnector job that delivered the rendered file to the recipient.

How to assemble a scene. Pass a <SidebarMock /> as the sidebar prop and a list of { node: <WidgetMock />, pos: 'top-left' } entries as the widgets prop. Six positions are available (top-left, top-right, middle-left, middle-right, bottom-left, bottom-right). Each comes with a small built-in rotation so the composition reads as playful, not gridded.

The component lives in docusaurus-preset/src/components/MockScene/. Pass compact for tighter scenes inside cards.
