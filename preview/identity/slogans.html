Triggers · why an SMB decision-maker would click

Hero, lede, first eight seconds

Wij sturen geen sales na een issue, alleen een commit.

We do not send sales after an issue, only a commit.

The OSS-credibility trigger. For the developer and IT lead who have "open source" as a requirement and know it's usually a half-truth.

Developer · SMB New

Gebruik Conduction zonder ooit met Conduction te praten.

Use Conduction without ever talking to Conduction.

For the buyer who's had enough of demo gates and sales cycles. We build products, not funnels. Literally: download, install, done.

SMB · Developer foundation.html

Geen workshop. Geen lock-in.

No methodology workshop. No lock-in.

Two single sentences that name the two biggest fears of an SMB buyer: the implementation project and vendor lock. Literally, that they don't have to.

SMB landing.html

Twee minuten installeren. Admin-rechten vereist.

Two-minute install. Admin required.

Concrete minute count plus the only barrier. Not "fast" or "simple" as adjectives, but a measurement plus a precondition.

SMB · Developer hero.html

Je verwijdert 'm net zo makkelijk als je 'm installeert.

The day you uninstall is as easy as the day you installed.

The anti-lock-in promise in action form. Not "no lock-in" (a claim), but the proof: try it, throw it away if you don't like it.

SMB · Developer app-detail.html

Gratis apps. Optionele support. Geen upgrade-nags.

Free apps. Optional support. No upgrade nags.

Three SaaS-fatigue pain points in three sentences. The buyer recognises themselves in all three and immediately knows this is a different model.
