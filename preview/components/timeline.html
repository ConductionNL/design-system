Design system  /  Components  /  Timeline
Timeline + Milestone · vertical year-by-year story strip with a pulsing “now” marker
2019

Conduction B.V. founded.

Started in Amsterdam as a Dutch government consultancy, building one-off ZGW integrations for gemeenten.

2021

Common Ground & Common Gateway.

Built our own open-source framework for the Dutch Common Ground architecture: a Common Gateway that gemeenten could plug into instead of yet another point-to-point integration.

2025

Pivot to Nextcloud.

Dropped Common Gateway as our primary stack. The lessons (typed registers, open APIs, federated catalogues) became the foundation of OpenRegister, OpenCatalogi and OpenConnector.

2026

ConNext.

Twelve apps brought under one product brand. connext.conduction.nl launches as the public surface; Conduction stays the company name behind the contracts.

Now

Shipping.

Open roadmap on GitHub, weekly stable releases, partner program ramping up. The story isn't finished, every sprint adds another app or a bigger customer.

React / MDX usage

Mount in MDX as <Timeline/> + <Milestone/>

Vertical year-by-year story strip with a gradient axis line (orange-fading-to-cobalt at the endpoints) and a pointy-top hex marker per milestone. The current milestone gets now for a slow pulse so the timeline reads as still-going. Used on /about for the company origin story; reusable on /roadmap and any other history-style page.

Import & example

import {Timeline, Milestone} from '@conduction/docusaurus-preset/components';

<Timeline>
  <Milestone year="2019" title="Conduction B.V. founded.">
    Started in Amsterdam as a Dutch government consultancy...
  </Milestone>
  <Milestone year="2026" title="ConNext.">
    Twelve apps brought under one product brand...
  </Milestone>
  <Milestone year="Now" title="Shipping." now>
    Open roadmap on GitHub, weekly stable releases...
  </Milestone>
</Timeline>

Props

  • children one or more <Milestone/> nodes

Milestone takes year (string, rendered as mono-uppercase), title (h4), now (boolean, animates the hex marker with a pulse), and children (the paragraph body).
