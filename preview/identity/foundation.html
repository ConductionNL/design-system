What we build · apps, not engagements

The shift: from project shop to product company.

Historically Conduction's work came out of projects, training, and advice — engagements where a customer hired us to build, implement, or guide. That work still exists, but it's not what Conduction is about anymore.

Conduction is a product company. We build an ecosystem of cooperating Nextcloud apps — and customers can use them without ever talking to us.

Download from the Nextcloud app store, install, done. All apps are always free and open source — no Community/Enterprise split, no feature-gating, no "Pro" tier.

We earn our keep through two supporting layers around the ecosystem — see right. Both are secondary, never leading: every brand surface (website, print, decks) leads with the apps and the ecosystem. Support and Services are explained honestly where relevant, never as a forced funnel or upgrade pressure.

Revenue layer · 1

Support

Optional safety net on top of the free software. Helpdesk plus proactive telemetry-based outreach. Two tiers — Standard and Premium — billed per app × per user × per year.

"Support" in customer language · "SLA" only in contract language
Revenue layer · 2

Services

Project-based work with a published rate card. Development €125/hr · Consultancy €150/hr · Training (online: free, on-site: €1,000–1,200 per half-day) · Certification per certificate. Optional €15K strippenkaart with 10% discount.

For customers whose needs go beyond install + Support
