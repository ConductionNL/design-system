Why we built it this way

Three steps, no feature tour

Welcome emails are famous for "Welcome to our platform!! Here are 12 features to discover!!" Our version: three concrete steps, one button, link to the community. First-week success, not first-impression feel-good.

  • "$ man {{slug}}" eyebrow. Plex Mono, terminal-style. Matches the Conduction CLI heritage and signals: this is a tool, not a SaaS product with an onboarding flow.
  • Hex-numbered steps. Step 3 is mint green (community), the other two cobalt. Visual cue: step 3 is "human", the others are "tool".
  • One CTA, not four. "Start in {{app}}". Not "Read our blog / Follow on Twitter / Share with colleagues / Watch the webinar".
  • Slack and forum links direct. No "go to your dashboard and click through to..." MKB buyers want one click to where the people are.
  • "Reply to this mail. I read them all." Not "for support open a ticket at portal.x.com". Reply goes to a human.
