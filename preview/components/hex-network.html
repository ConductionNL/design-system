HexNetwork — the honeycomb-grid component.

Pointy-top hex tiles arranged in a 3-3-3 (or 2-3-2 / 3-4-3) honeycomb. One tile in the centre carries the brand anchor; the surrounding tiles carry integrations or partner logos. Two surfaces, one component: the ConNext integrations row and the partner-page hero.

SURFACE 01 · Integrations on ConNext

Plays well with the workspace.

Conduction apps live next to the standard Nextcloud tools, not instead of them. Mail, calendar, contacts, Talk, Decks. Plus the wider workspace stack we ship next to: XWiki for docs, OpenProject for tasks, Discourse for the public forum.
