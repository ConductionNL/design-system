10 · Identity / Products

Apps. Solutions. One vocabulary.

An app is software we ship. A solution is what a customer achieves with it. Don't conflate.

Terminology · the apps-vs-solutions distinction
A
App

Software we ship.

Concrete, installable, one-click from the Nextcloud app store. Has a name, a version, a maintainer, a GitHub repo.

e.g. OpenCatalogi · OpenRegister · OpenConnector · DocuDesk

S
Solution

What a customer achieves.

A concrete outcome — a problem solved, a regulation met, a workflow live. Built from apps but isn't itself an app.

e.g. WOO compliance · Organisatieregister · Zaakafhandeling

One app can contribute to many solutions. One solution often combines several apps. We talk from both sides — apps for visitors who know what they want, solutions for visitors who have a problem.

Never write "our WOO app" — there isn't one. Write "our WOO solution, built on OpenCatalogi, OpenConnector, and DocuDesk."
