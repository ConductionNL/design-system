01 · Title

Title slide

Cobalt-900 ground, orange hex, headline + presenter line. Always opens the deck.

02 · Speaker introduction

Speaker introduction

Hex avatar plus name, role, "background / nu / vraag mij over" labels. The consultant is a person, not an avatar.

03 · Agenda

Agenda

Numbered hex bullets, 3 to 6 items. Use to set expectations and to navigate back to mid-deck.

04 · Content

Content

The workhorse. H2 + body copy or bullets. Underused on purpose, reach for two-column or app spotlight first.

05 · Two-column

Two-column

Compare, contrast, before/after. Headers stacked above each column; equal weight.

“Eindelijk één plek…”
— Marleen, gemeente X
06 · Quote

Full-bleed quote

Customer voice. White text on cobalt, attribution muted. Used sparingly, never two in a row.

07 · App spotlight

App spotlight

Hex glyph left, name + tagline + three bullets right. One slide per app. Reuse for partner spotlight.

08 · Comparison

Comparison

Mint vs terracotta panels. "Met Conduction" / "Zonder Conduction" or pros/cons. Honest, not gloating.

09 · Logo wall

Customer / partner logos

Eight to twelve marks, equal size. Greyscale by default, colour only on the hovered/featured one.
