Partners ship ConNext at scale.

Hosters, integrators, and Nextcloud-strategic agencies who deploy and run our apps for customers we can't reach alone. Three tiers — Partner, Certified, Strategic — each with a clear bar to clear and a clear thing they unlock.

Three tiers

Pick the partner that fits the engagement.

Partner

Partner.

Has installed ConNext apps in production at one or more customers. Self-trained on the docs. Public listing in the directory.

Certified

Certified.

Engineering team has passed our certification on at least three core apps. Customers see the gold avatar in the directory and on quotes.

  • Everything in Partner
  • Certified gold avatar on directory
  • Co-branded case-study slot
  • Pre-release access (one minor)
Strategic

Strategic.

Joint roadmap commitments and shared engineering. Reserved for a small number of partners — by invitation, with quarterly business reviews.

  • Everything in Certified
  • Joint roadmap planning
  • Engineering hours from Conduction
  • Co-sale on government tenders
