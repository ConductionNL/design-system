Design system  /  Components  /  Tier explain
TierExplain · three-column tier-comparison row (used on /support and /partners)
Host

Host partner.

Ships our open-source apps to customers. Sells, hosts, and resells Nextcloud-supported variants through their own channels. No SLA with Conduction.

  • Resells apps on own channel
  • Sets own pricing
  • No direct line to Conduction support
Service

Service partner.

Holds an SLA with Conduction. Their team can call us directly for third-line support, with named contacts and input on bug priority and feature requests.

  • Named Conduction contacts
  • Third-line support SLA
  • Input on bug priority + roadmap
Certified

Certified partner.

Trained, audited, and on the joint roadmap with Conduction. Eligible to co-sale on public tenders with our products, with the Conduction-credential mark on their own collateral.

  • Joint roadmap participation
  • Tender co-sale eligibility
  • Conduction-credential mark on collateral
React / MDX usage

Mount in MDX as <TierExplain/> + <TierCard/>

Three-up explanation row for the partner-tier hierarchy. Each card carries a tier pill (cobalt-700 for Host, gold for Service, cobalt-blue for Certified), a sentence-case title, a lede, and a short benefits list. Used on /support, /partners, and /commonground.

Import & example

import {TierExplain, TierCard} from '@conduction/docusaurus-preset/components';

<TierExplain>
  <TierCard tier="host" title="Host partner.">
    Ships our open-source apps to customers...
    <ul>
      <li>Resells apps on own channel</li>
      <li>Sets own pricing</li>
    </ul>
  </TierCard>
  <TierCard tier="service" title="Service partner.">...</TierCard>
  <TierCard tier="certified" title="Certified partner.">...</TierCard>
</TierExplain>

Props

  • tier one of host / service / certified — tints the tier pill (cobalt / gold / blue)
  • title sentence-case headline
  • children body content; usually a paragraph + an <ul> of bullet points (mint hex bullet automatic)

Tier slug names match the canonical PartnerCard + PartnerSidecard taxonomy introduced in @conduction/docusaurus-preset@2.0.0.
