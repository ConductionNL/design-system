FAQ.

If the apps are free, what am I paying for?

Time and a phone number. Support buys you a contracted response window, a named human to reach, and the engineering hours behind hot-fixes and design reviews. The apps themselves stay open-source, EUPL-licensed, and free — that doesn't change between tiers.

Can I switch tiers without re-installing?

Yes. Tiers are a contract layer; the apps keep running unchanged. Upgrade or downgrade with one calendar month's notice.

Do you sell hosting?

No. ConNext apps run on your Nextcloud — managed by you, by your hoster, or by a Nextcloud Strategic partner. We point you at the right one if you don't have one yet.

What's the licence?

EUPL-1.2 — the European Union Public Licence. Compatible with GPL/AGPL, recognised by Dutch government tenders, and explicit about contribution back to the commons. The full text ships with every app.

How fast does Strategic actually respond?

Median first-response time over the last twelve months: 38 minutes during NL+BE office hours. The 4-hour figure is the contractual ceiling, not the average.
