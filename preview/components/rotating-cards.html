RotatingCards — auto-advancing capability rotator.

One card on a cobalt-900 stage, swapping every five seconds. Hover to pause, click a hex pip to jump. Reference: honeycomb.io home "What Honeycomb helps you do", in Conduction tokens.

What Conduction helps you do

Six things, in two minutes.

Hover the card to pause. Use the hex pips to jump to a specific capability. The rotator pauses on focus too, so screen readers can finish reading the active card before it advances.

The component is <RotatingCards eyebrow title cards=[{icon,eyebrow,title,summary,cta}] interval=5000 />. Hover or focus pauses the auto-advance; left/right arrow keys step manually. The static preview above re-implements the rotation in vanilla JS so the demo runs without a build step.
