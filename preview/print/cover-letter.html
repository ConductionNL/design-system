Why we built it this way

The letterhead is the chrome, the cover letter is the words

letterhead.html ships the header + footer + brand tells. cover-letter.html ships the actual copy: how a Conduction letter sounds, with placeholder blocks you replace for each send.

  • Three paragraphs, not four. Opening, body, sign-off. Reader scans; reader decides; reader responds. Four paragraphs dilute.
  • Short subject, verb-led. "Offer: WOO publication" not "Subject: offer regarding the establishment of a publication environment".
  • "Hi {{first name}}", never "Dear Sir/Madam". MKB-direct. If you want to write formally, don't write the letter.
  • Concrete next step. No "we'd love to hear from you". Date, moment, name. Someone can act after reading.
  • Template, not an example. Placeholders with {{ }}, search-replace ready. For an Acme version, see quotation.
